AsurSura is Lithuanian artist that found her passion for drawing later in years. Studied glass art but found herself in Digital art. She is not afraid to draw shocking, grotesque images. She has her own take on social problems trough art. Unique style of drawing that shows so much attention to every detail; even smallest object has a purpose in drawings composition.

Every single portrait drawn represents the drawn personâ€™s personality in one way or another. There are symbols and submittal messages about what kind of person he is, from the artist perspective, public or his own. Only by looking at the portrait you can paint a picture in your head what kind of person he is. This only shows the power of picture.

"Mother" - goddess in the bird's body. Perfect looking woman who protects home's fireplace. One of the life stages, when being a mother and a good wife becomes the main goal in life.
"Former faces" - person creates and replaces a lot of social roles through life. In old age, a past is being viewed; all stories consist of houses in different cities, where every building has its own story.

The identity of a person who combines various social roles. Everyone feels the need to know himself, to separate himself from others and to be assigned to specific social segments. Identity is divided into psychological and social identities. There are a lot of personal identities; I have focused only on their paths and looked at it through the feminine prism.
