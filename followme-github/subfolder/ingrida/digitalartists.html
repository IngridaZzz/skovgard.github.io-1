Digital art is an artistic work or practice that uses digital technology as an essential part of the creative or presentation process. After some initial resistance, the impact of digital technology has transformed activities such as painting, drawing, sculpture, while new forms, such as net art, digital installation art have become recognized artistic practices. More generally the term digital artist is used to describe an artist who makes use of digital technologies in the production of art. In an expanded sense, "digital art" is contemporary art that uses the methods of mass production or digital media.
